## Why speed-to-lead matters

Research on lead response is brutal and consistent: the odds of qualifying a lead drop off a cliff after the first few minutes. Contact a lead within 5 minutes and you're vastly more likely to convert than a competitor who calls back an hour later. Most businesses respond in *hours* — and quietly lose the deal to whoever answered first.

This calculator turns that into a dollar figure for your business: plug in your lead volume, close rate, and average deal size, and it estimates the revenue leaking out through slow response.

## What is speed-to-lead?

Speed-to-lead is the time between a prospect raising their hand (form fill, call, chat) and your business making first contact. It's one of the highest-leverage, most-ignored levers in local marketing. More on the concept: https://thinxster.com/what-is-speed-to-lead
